Pilgrimage Planning Tips

  • Best Season: Most temples are best visited during October to March when weather is pleasant
  • Accommodation: Plan ahead during festival seasons for better availability
  • Transportation: Buses, trains, and flights connect major pilgrimage destinations
  • Dress Code: Wear modest, respectful clothing when visiting temples
  • Health: Carry necessary medicines and stay hydrated during pilgrimages
  • Guides: Hire local guides for better understanding of temple history and rituals
  • Darshan Live: Catch live aarti and ceremonies on our Live Darshan page before planning your visit
